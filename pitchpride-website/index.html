How It Started

The PitchPride Story

Every great journey begins with a single step. For PitchPride, that journey began in 2025 with one simple but powerful vision — to provide affordable football jerseys to passionate fans across Fiji.

It all started with our founder and director, Mr. Naashit Hussain, who purchased his very first jersey — a customized Real Madrid jersey. What seemed like an ordinary purchase quickly turned into a lifelong passion. With every new jersey added to his personal collection, his love for football culture and authentic designs grew stronger.

As his collection expanded, so did an idea.

Mr. Hussain realized that many Fijian football fans shared the same passion but often struggled to find premium jerseys at reasonable prices. Rather than keeping his passion to himself, he decided to turn it into something meaningful for the community.

That idea became PitchPride.

Operating through a convenient pre-order online model, PitchPride began importing a wide range of football jerseys — from club and international teams to player editions and fan versions — making them accessible to supporters throughout Fiji without the high retail prices.

From day one, our mission has remained unchanged:

To bring the world's best football jerseys closer to every Fijian fan, without compromising on quality or affordability.

Today, PitchPride continues to grow, driven by the trust and support of our customers. Every order placed is another step toward building something bigger than just a jersey business — it's about connecting fans with the teams and players they love.

Looking Ahead

This is only the beginning. Our vision for the future is to expand PitchPride into one of Fiji's leading sportswear brands by:

  • Offering an even wider range of football merchandise
  • Providing faster and more reliable service
  • Enhancing the overall customer experience
  • Continuing to deliver premium-quality jerseys at affordable prices

At PitchPride, we believe every jersey tells a story — and ours began with one football fan who dared to dream.

Know Your Kit

Player Version vs. Fan Version

For the Pitch

Player / Match Edition

Same as what players wear during actual matches.

FabricThin, lightweight — advanced moisture-wicking
BadgeHeat-applied flat badge, no raised edges
FitSlim athletic cut visible in the silhouette
VentilationLaser-cut ventilation zones on fabric
TechNike Dri-FIT ADV, Adidas HEAT.RDY, Puma ULTRAWEAVE
PriceMore expensive
For the Stands

Fan Version

Made for supporters to wear casually or at games.

FabricThicker, durable — standard polyester weave
BadgeEmbroidered raised badge with visible stitching
FitRelaxed regular fit silhouette
VentilationBasic breathable fabric, no laser-cut zones
TechBasic sweat-wicking (Nike Dri-FIT, not ADV)
PriceMore affordable
